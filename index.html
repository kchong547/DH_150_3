A little about me...

My name is Katherine Chong and I'm a third year pursuing computer science at University of California Los Angeles. I really love utilizing my imagination and bringing my thoughts to life, whether it be through drawing or coding. I'm happiest when I'm blending my creative abilities and my interest in tech together to create something new and exciting! I think one of the most intriguing things about computer science is its infinite possibilies and applications, which makes it easy to blend with any interests.

Creating an impact in a community or in someone's life is a goal I hold dear to my heart. Currently, I'm the External Vice President for Association for Computing Machinery (ACM at UCLA), the largest computer science student organization at UCLA for the 2020-2021 term. I'm also involved in exploretech.la, a student-led organization that aims to expose underserved Title 1 high school students in LAUSD to opportunities in technology. Find out more about what I do in these orgs here!

When I'm not occupied by academics or coding, I enjoy testing the limits of my artistic ability. Take a look some of my recent art and projects! In my free time, I also love being inspired by music, watching shows, reading books, or taking a nice walk outdoors.
